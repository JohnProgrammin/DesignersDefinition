Our Story

Founded in Abuja in 2018, Designer's Definition emerged from a passion for blending contemporary African aesthetics with timeless luxury. Our founder, Adeola Banks, envisioned a brand that would redefine Nigerian fashion for the global stage.

Each piece is meticulously crafted in our atelier by skilled artisans who share our commitment to excellence. The signature gold accents in our collections pay homage to Nigeria's rich cultural heritage while maintaining a modern sensibility.

We believe true luxury lies in the details - from the precision of our stitching to the ethical sourcing of our materials. This philosophy has made us the choice of discerning clients across West Africa.
